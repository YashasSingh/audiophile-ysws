Pick Your Build Path

Track 01

Hardware Builder

Design and build physical audio gear: visualizers, MP3 players, headphone mods, DAC boards, or entirely new devices. Tracking is based on update posts whenever something significant happens.

  • Funding goes to project parts
  • Ship updates every 1-5 hours of work
  • Progress verified via update posts

Track 02

Software Builder

Build audio drivers, DSP tooling, plugins, and creative audio systems. Tracking is based on Hack Club Hackatime hours, with optional update posts.

  • Hackatime logs verified work hours
  • Updates optional but welcome
  • IEM tiers unlocked by hours

Requirements

  • You must be 13-18 years old. Hack Club is a teen community.
  • Your project can follow a tutorial, but it must show uniqueness.
  • Software tools must have a real purpose. No farming hours for better IEMs.
  • Only 10 percent AI allowed. I will know if you are LARPING.
  • You must join the updates channel: Audiophile YSWS Slack.
  • Open source everything.
