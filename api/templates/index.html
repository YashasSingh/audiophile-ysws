What is Tick Tock?

The Challenge

Design and build a custom pocket watch by combining PCB engineering, mechanical CAD design, and hands-on assembly. It's a 7-hour deep dive into hardware engineering.

The Process

Follow structured modules: schematic capture, PCB routing, case design, and assembly. We provide templates, guides, and direct support to help you succeed.

The Reward

Your custom PCB manufactured, your case 3D printed, and a component kit to bring it to life. Plus, your design becomes part of the Tick Tock gallery.

The Maker's Rules

Two key requirements to maintain integrity and document your journey

The Lapse Requirement

All work must be recorded using time-lapse software (Lapse or similar). This provides irrefutable "proof of work"—a visual journey from blank canvas to finished assembly.

Why: We want to celebrate your journey and verify the work is yours.

The Maker's Journal

Keep a live journal during the build with 3–5 short entries (1–2 sentences each). Reflect on challenges faced or breakthroughs made during specific chunks of the design.

Why: Engineering is iterative; your journal captures that growth.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience?

No! Tick Tock is designed for hardware novices while remaining challenging for veteran makers. We provide comprehensive guides and templates to get you started.

What tools do I need?

For schematic and PCB: KiCad (free & open-source). For CAD: Fusion 360 (free for students) or similar. For time-lapse: Any smartphone with a time-lapse app or dedicated software like Lapse.

How much does it cost?

Tick Tock covers manufacturing of your PCB and 3D printing of your case, plus a curated component kit. You only pay for any custom filament colors beyond the default.

Can I take longer than 7 hours?

Absolutely! The 7-hour requirement is the minimum. If you spend extra time, you'll unlock additional component tiers and extended grant eligibility.

What happens after I submit?

We verify your Lapse video and journal, review your designs, and manufacture your PCB and case. Shipping typically takes 2-3 weeks once production is complete.

Can I showcase my build?

Yes! Your Lapse video may be featured on our TikTok/Instagram, and you'll be part of the Tick Tock gallery. All showcases are optional—it's your call.
